Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, MHCI, was held on August 21 - 23, 2018, in Madrid, Spain. MHCI is one of the 5 conferences that make up the World Congress on Electrical Engineering and Computer Systems and Science (EECSS'18). Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the conference becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.
